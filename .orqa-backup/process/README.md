The process layer is the knowledge maturity pipeline. Raw observations (lessons) are distilled into understanding (decisions), then into principles (pillars), then into practice (skills), then into enforcement (rules), and finally into verification records that prove enforcement is working.

Artifacts here are not consumed by work — they *govern* it. They are created by the pipeline itself as patterns emerge and recur.
